Hangover

Well, yesterday a nice crew were on Tacvbo’s, 13 guys drinking beer and sharing some nice moments (as I remember the room on a clock-like turn): p4ola, zerver, royald, tuxfer, Odín, Little_Bear, MiG, Tato, Ramón Carazo, GnuOwned, Juan (AntiloOp’s cousin) and AntiloOp.

It was nice to meet some guys I have seen on pictures before, or talked on IRC like GnuOwned and AntiloOp.

Anyway I woke up this morning and I was so thirsty, my tongue was dry and my mouth in need of a beer, but I just drank some lemon water which my mom made yesterday: It is so cool that water when you need it, it is refreshing, like seeing a dolphin swim when you are in a boat (I always have associated the fact of a dolphin swimming in the ocean, with refreshing freedom ;)

Today is quite different from other days: There is a hot sun ;) The zone where I go to school and work is very near to the western on Mexico City, very close to Toluca (Toluca is well known as a very old city, its average is below 12 C), and right over there it’s been cold, raining, etc. So it is nice to see a pure and beautiful sun today.

Two days away from Brazil, and because you all have requested it ;), here is my TODO during my stay there:

o Finish the little port I am making of Borland’s conio C library, to a well structured and need-fit compliant.

o Help on debian-installer l10n/i18n issues.

o Get involved on Debian Desktop Project.

o Get my packages to the Debian Archive.

o Work a little bit on linevents.

o As somebody on the DebConf4 mailing list said: “Expect the unexpected” ;-)

o Probably I will find excuses for not to do these and others things :-P
