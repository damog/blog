World Cup just around the corner

Well, Mexico played three preparation games in Europe on their way to start the World Cup. They played against France and the Netherlands on their territories and well, I think Mexico was not ready enough, it is not solid and the manager was making lots of crazy movements. They ended 1-0 and 2-1, respectively. Obviously those were difficult matches against fútbol potences on their home, but still Mexico has plenty of capacity to beat any of those countries.

The third game was against a German university, Gottingen, which was more a diplomatic match (the manager and players didn’t want to play it, but it was because of the chief managers of Mexico, trying to make a good relationship with the city and/or Germany, itself). We won 0-3.

Next week, on Friday, the World Cup will begin. The Mexican team will be playing firstly, against Iran, then Angola and finally Portugal, on group D. I don’t see Mexico solid and trustable enough yet, but we’ll see how it goes against Iran, which have been playing widely good (they won Costa Rica 3-2, back in March; drew 2-2 with Croatia and won 5-2 against Bosnia-Herzegovina; these last two in the last couple of days). Iran is not at all, an easy enemy.

We’ll see how it goes. I’ll be reporting more World Cup news on the next weeks :)

Wed May 31, 2006 David Moreno

Debian-related news on the wiki front page

I commented Joey, on DebConf, about an idea on bringing Debian-News.net news syndication into the front page of the Debian Wiki, on some similar way as Wikipedia displays current news on their main page. After thinking a bit more on the idea, and after actually receiving a green light on the Debian Wiki BOF, I thought it would be better if we could include more than only one feed, so we could include more Debian-related news sites, like Debian Administration, DebCentral, etc, as well as some other official feeds like the DSAs or the DWNs.

So, after setting a local Planet up and writing a little script, we ended up with the Latest official and unofficial Debian-related news box on the wiki FrontPage, which I hope people would like.

Further information about this is can be found at the FrontPageNews page.

El problema de la cafetería y las niñas tontas de la Ibero

Un problema de razonamiento matemático.

Tres niñas de Comunicaciones de la Ibero están tomando cafés Capuccino Latte en el Cubo (cafetería central de dicha universidad). Al pedir la cuenta, el meserete les indica que por los tres cafés son 25 pesos. Cada una de las chamacas, abriendo su bolso Louis Vuitton pone 10 pesos, es decir, entre las tres ponen 30 pesos. Como sobran 5 pesos, cada una toma un peso de vuelta, y los dos pesos restantes los dejan de propina. Es decir, cada una paga 9 pesos, que multiplicados por 3 serían 27, más los 2 de propina, 29. ¿Qué pasó con el otro peso? :)

Las niñas tontas de la Ibero no lo pudieron resolver y mejor se fueron a clase de Comunicación Audiovisual Retroposmoderna del Pensamiento de Tendencia Neoyorkina del Barroco Recientetardío.

Si alguien deja la respuesta de este problema, no se vale leer los comentarios hasta que cada uno lo resuelva ;)

While reading the available documentation on Joey Hess’s ikiwiki, I started to see his pics on previous DebConfs. It’s amazing how quick things can happen. It seemed to me DebConf5 happened last week or something and we are now facing the Mexican DebConf in a couple of days. :D

It’s true that I wasn’t able to help as I wished on the organization people, because of my moving to Venezuela in the last couple of months, but DebConf, and everything around it, is starting this week for me. No porque...

No recuerdo si leí en su blog o escuché en el podcast del Warpig la siguiente frase, sobre bandas alternativas de rock: “No porque sean underground, tienen que sonar de la mierda”. La cual es una frase que aplica en tantas situaciones de la vida, pongo aquí mis ejemplos.

“No porque sea software libre, tienen que estar escrito de la mierda”. “No porque sea software libre, tiene que ser una mierda”. “No porque sean indígenas, se les tiene que tratar de la mierda”. “No porque sean novatos, se libran de los periodicazos”. “No porque te creas un cabrón, dejas de ser mierda”. “No porque hables a mis espaldas, dejas de ser mierda”. “No porque tengas huevitos, tienes ídem para hablar de frente”. “No porque me hayas ofrecido alguna vez una mano, dejas de ser una mierda”. “No porque seas un escritor alternativo, debes oler a mierda”. “No por parecer algo, mereces todo en el mundo”. “No porque te trataron feo en la primaria, debes tirar mierda en la universidad”. “No porque lo hagas en tus ratos libres, tiene que ser una mierda”.

Lindo :)
