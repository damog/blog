Several things

Sun 27 Jun 2004 02:17:14 PM UTC

Well, yeah. Lots of things has happened on the last days. Let’s start without particular order.

* My mother went ill because of pressure and stress. She, fortunately, is getting better. I have to start doing some things by myself, which she used to do.

* My DSL modem was fucked up by a ray storm. The thing is, the only thing that was fucked up was the ethernet port… It’s insane, because the USB port is still running. There is no other explanation, the storm made my modem sad :-( Anyway, MiG had another DSL modem without using it, so he lent it to me, thank you MiG ;) I’m online again :)

* Yesterday, GnuOwned, MiG and I went to Cuernavaca on my car. Our conversation deciding on going was something like this:

Damog: The northern point of the globe I’ve been in is Tepic, Nayarit.
GnuOwned (which is from Los Cabos, BCS): Actually, the southern point I’ve been in is this, Mexico City.
Damog: So you don’t even know Cuernavaca?
GnuOwned: No.
Damog: What about going there now? ;-)
GnuOwned: Ok! ;D

So, we went to pick up MiG (we called Tacvbo up, but he was busy with his mother and sister) and toke way to Cuernavaca, which is a very nice city, clean, beautiful and the weather is just great. I wouldn’t have any trouble on moving there.

* And yes, I’ve been missing her too much, more than I could imagine.
