Week in Guadalajara

Sun 26 Sep 2004 06:06:31 PM UTC

This incoming week will be madness. Two main things will happen.

I’m giving a talk at Festival de Software Libre in Gualadajara, on thrusday. It will be about colaboration in free software projects, focused on Debian.

The other important thing to do is a physics exam on Friday, so it will be madness to cover both things, and try to not lose more classes than the ones I will, so this is my plan:

  • Monday 27 th. I have to finish a homework for Tuesday, and it is long. I only have one day left for deliver it, so the entire day I have to work on it.
  • Tuesday 28 th. Deliver my homework at 9 am, hopefully finished. Stay on that class until the end (around 11 am). Go the office (FYI I work in the university, so the office is pretty close from the classroom) and pick up my stuff (I’ve already informed I was missing almost the entire week).

    Get on the bus with Tacvbo at 12:30 pm. Then we’d spend 7⁄8 hours within the bus until we reach Guadalajara.

  • Wednesday 29 th. Watch Tacvbo’s talk about Gentoo and scream about its disadvantages . The entire day, I would spend it finishing my presentation.
  • Thrusday 30 th. Give my talk at 11 am. Eat lunch at midday. Take a bus around 4 pm in order to return to Mexico City. In the meantime, being in the bus, I have to study physics for my exam, that will be the only moment alone where I’m going to be able to study. Arriving estimated time: Midnight.
  • Friday 1st Oct. Wake up at 6 am for my Calculus class at 7, and my Physics exam at 9. Then I would go to work. Then, go to eat and spend the noon with Rachel.

    That’s it. That is evil plan for this week. I hope I could post activities everyday. See ya!
