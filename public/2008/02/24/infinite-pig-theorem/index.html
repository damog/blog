Infinite Pig Theorem!

Sun 24 Feb 2008 06:28:00 PM UTC

So, I have finally moved my blog to MovableType from Wordpress and a couple of interesting things have happened:

  • I’ve gave birth to my new blog, Infinite Pig Theorem.
  • I’ve started Blog::Normalize as an entirely i-don’t-give-too-much-shit-if-people-disagree-so-beat-it-dude project. You can import posts to MovableType from Wordpress as the time of writing which was my whole purpose at first, but then expanded the idea.

It feels good to be back.
