debaday

Wed 14 Sep 2005 05:55:49 AM UTC

I’ve been working lately on trying to bring back the old debaday effort.

Debaday was a little nice idea by Andrew Sweger writing a bit of one different and interesting Sarge package daily, started on March 2004. In Novembre 2004, his debaday blog died.

Since January I’ve been wanting to bring this idea back and last week I finally found some real time to do it.

The new debaday: http://debaday.debian.net/.

The hosting is kindly offered by the free comunity, nipl. Thanks, guys. Contributions and any kind of suggestions are appreciated and welcome.
