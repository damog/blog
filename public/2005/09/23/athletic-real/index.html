Fri Sep 23, 2005 David Moreno

Athletic - Real

Unfortunately, when we arrived to his flat, he
turned on the TV and there was a replay of the game on ESPN, so we saw
the match was 1-1 in minute 60. Crap, 2/3 of the game spoiled. Anyway,
we saw the whole 60 minutes (with beer, without it it would have been
impossible :)). After the 60 minutes, Real Madrid scored two times
more, so they ended 3-1.

Since I’m not a huge Spanish fútbol fan,
I was cheering Athletic with him. After the match he was swearing with
a funny Spaniard accent: “¡Jodeeeeeeeer, me cago en la putaaaaaa! ¡Putos fascistas de mieeeeeeeeeerda!”. But the funniest part was swearing Mexican style: “¡Que chingue a su madre, el pinche Real Madrid!”. ;-)

He’s
a great guy. If I ever visit Spain again, I’ll definitively visit
him in the Basque country (if he happens to be back in La Madre Patria).
