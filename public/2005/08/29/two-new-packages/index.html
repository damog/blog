Two new packages

Mon 29 Aug 2005 05:24:13 AM UTC

I uploaded two new packages today, GParted and fteqcc.

Random thoughts:

  • In the next days, I’ll be using some of the patches made available in the CVS for gparted. The version that’ll hit unstable is raw 0.0.8.
  • fteqcc is the FTE QuakeC compiler, which between other things, will make possible for nexuiz to enter the archive. This was the first step to bring nexuiz officially to Debian.
  • Being suscribed to debian-wnpp@lists.debian.org is quite fun and you always can help a bit people dealing with wnpp bugs.
  • Pretending you don’t need glasses doesn’t work anymore. I have received the ones I ordered last week today and now I can see clearly, heh.
  • I’m getting ready to start my portuguese lessons on the Brazilian embassy in middle September.
