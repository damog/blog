Trip to Central America

I have started planning (and saving) a trip through Central America.


I’d be flying to Panama City. Then San José, Managua, Tegucigalpa and
Guatemala. And then back to Mexico City. The only country I would not
visit in Central America because it is out of the route is Belize.


I’m getting excited because of this. I hope somebody would like to join me. I hate being alone when traveling.
