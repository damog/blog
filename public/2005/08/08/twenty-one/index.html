Twenty-one

A day like today, but 21 years ago, I was born. Random thoughts:

  • I hosted a birthday party last friday. All my dearest friends were there.

  • I’m now able to drink with abrotman in Philadelphia! ;-)

  • It sucks that my back-to-school day is my birthday.


  • Tato, one of my high school homies, will also be 21 on the 11st.

  • I felt asleep on my own party last friday because I was too drunk
    (at 1:30 am). My guests left around 4 or 5. That happens when you are
    still 20 ;-)

  • I watched a The Simpsons episode today where Bart is feeling he is not a kid anymore.

  • This is actually the beginning of my third decade of life. And saying that sounds like 30s.

  • I’m getting old very quickly.
