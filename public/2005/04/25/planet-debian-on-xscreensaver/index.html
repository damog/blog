Planet Debian on xscreensaver

Mon 25 Apr 2005 07:03:00 PM UTC

The other day, at work, I was fooling around the lab (I work on common area called ‘lab’ where the different products of the company I work for are assembled) and while looking at any random point I was surprised to see my machine with a black background, huge terminal-like green fonts displaying: “Paul van Tilburg: 25th birthday…”. Shit! Mozillion has cracked up my box! :-)

But after reading a little bit more, I was happy to realize one of the screensavers on xscreensaver has an RSS p.d.o reader :-) That is indeed very, very nice.
