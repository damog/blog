Planeta Debian en Español

Recently, a proposal by Alejandro Ríos was made in debian-devel-spanish to provide a Debian planet in Spanish to syndicate blogs from developers, maintainers and contributors in that language.

After working a bit (and after my trip to Cuba), we are able to show this to the public. I wish more people contributing with Debian writing blogs in Spanish, could ask their addition by writing to the mailing list; we are only 10 persons there so far.

Join us! http://planeta.debian.net/
