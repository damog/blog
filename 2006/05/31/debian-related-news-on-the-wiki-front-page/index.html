Debian-related news on the wiki front page

I commented Joey, on DebConf, about an idea on bringing Debian-News.net news syndication into the front page of the Debian Wiki, on some similar way as Wikipedia displays current news on their main page. After thinking a bit more on the idea, and after actually receiving a green light on the Debian Wiki BOF, I thought it would be better if we could include more than only one feed, so we could include more Debian-related news sites, like Debian Administration, DebCentral, etc, as well as some other official feeds like the DSAs or the DWNs.

So, after setting a local Planet up and writing a little script, we ended up with the Latest official and unofficial Debian-related news box on the wiki FrontPage, which I hope people would like.

Further information about this is can be found at the FrontPageNews page.
